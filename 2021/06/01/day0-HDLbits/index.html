Gatesv100

短版 #bit操作
given a 100-bit input vector in[99:0],

  • out_both: out_both[98] = in[98] & in[99]
  • out_any: out_any[2] = in[1] | in[0]
  • out_different: out_different[98] = in[98] ^ in[99], but wrapping around.
